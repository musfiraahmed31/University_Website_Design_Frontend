Who We Are

About Our Institution

Located in the heart of a rapidly evolving global landscape, Pak-Austria Fachhochschule stands as a beacon of excellence in applied sciences and technology education. We are committed to transforming Pakistan's manufacturing and technology sectors through innovative education and research.

Our institution embodies a major paradigm shift in educational approach, focusing on knowledge-based industrialization and creating a competitive industry culture. Through state-of-the-art facilities and world-class faculty, we prepare students for tomorrow's challenges.

Our Vision

To emerge as a globally recognized leader in foreseeing and addressing techno-industrial demands and socio-economic challenges. We envision creating future leaders who drive innovation and sustainable development across industries.

Our Mission

To provide cutting-edge education through interactive learning, advanced laboratories, and smart classrooms. We are dedicated to fostering research excellence and building strong industry partnerships for practical learning experiences.

Core Values

Excellence, integrity, innovation, and collaboration form the foundation of our institution. We believe in creating an inclusive environment that nurtures talent, promotes ethical leadership, and celebrates diversity.

Strategic Direction

Our Strategic Goals

Driving excellence through focused objectives and measurable outcomes

Industrial Economy Development

Contributing to Pakistan's sound industrial economy through research, innovation, and skilled human resource development aligned with market needs.

Excellence in Higher Education

Achieving superior standards in engineering, science, and technology education through modern pedagogical approaches and continuous improvement.

Hi-Tech Industry Promotion

Paving pathways to success by fostering hi-tech industries and supporting technological advancement in critical sectors.

Technology Park Integration

Establishing an Integrated Technology Park with Business Incubation Centers to bridge academia and industry effectively.

World-Class Faculty

Engaging highly qualified faculty members well-versed in applied research and committed to student success and innovation.

Global Partnerships

Building strategic international collaborations for knowledge exchange, research cooperation, and student mobility programs.
